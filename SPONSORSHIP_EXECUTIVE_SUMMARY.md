# OHCS E-Library: Executive Summary

## AI-Powered Knowledge Platform for Ghana's Civil Service

---

### THE OPPORTUNITY

Ghana's 500,000+ civil servants across 50+ Ministries, Departments, and Agencies (MDAs) lack a unified digital platform for knowledge management, professional development, and collaboration. Critical documents are siloed, institutional knowledge is lost with retiring officers, and there is no structured support for employee wellness.

### PLATFORM AT A GLANCE

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Kwame AI Assistant** | Instant answers from official documents with citations |
| **Document Library** | 100,000+ document capacity with AI-powered search |
| **Learning Management** | Courses, certifications, and learning paths |
| **Ayo Wellness Counselor** | Anonymous AI mental health support with professional escalation |
| **Research Lab** | AI-assisted policy research and collaboration |
| **Collaboration Suite** | Forums, real-time chat, groups, and social networking |
| **Gamification** | XP, badges, leaderboards driving engagement |
